  • My Team

    We are a group of researchers, designers and developers who devote our effort in crafting solutions that best structure and guide the interaction between the human and the computer. We focus on products in large-scale business environment. Our goal is to apply innovative cutting-edge technology and solid research and field study on products for human to maximum the understanding of the computer's functionality and reveal the potential of human in increasing productivity and easing work experience using computer as a tool.

  • Design & Develop for Digitalization

    I mainly work on building a set of tools to digitalize both factory work process and shop management practice. This product is part of Siemens' digitalization process. As an addition to functionality-wise digitalization, our group wish to build workflow solutions with efficiency, consistent quality and good user experience.

    With Out Product

    The factory team(user) will

    • Reduce collaboration complexity
    • Improve work efficiency
    • Enhance shop safety
    • Elevate employers' work experience.

  • Half way through!

    Through fast-paced yet in-detailed research, now our group processes enormous amount of practical data and is finishing up the design of user flow for the entire application. We are ready to face any questions, concerns or challenges from the user and/or stake holder side. As of today, the major tasks for all team members are development oriented problem-solving and optimization. 85% local implementation is accomplished. We will be ready for migrating our database to cloud-based server very soon. We will coordinate with Siemens IT to finalize the security implementation, testing and distribution preparation before we push the product globally to all Siemens factory around the world.

    The research phase lasted for years. Multiple hardware platforms and software approaches were studied. Multiple prototypes were made and tested. My group went to the shop with our attempted solutions and collected user feedback face-to-face, then came back and studied feasibility for each platform and approach. After we decided the best practical solution, in-detail workflow design and implementation were initiated.

  • A Customized and Optimized Tool Set

    Above is a high-level abstraction of the design for user-oriented access customization. We design the tool set to meet practical requirement in the factory by identifying user groups and user roles(think of it like job title). Doing so also allows us to individually customize the design of workflow, usability for each use case as well as grant user access right accordingly.
